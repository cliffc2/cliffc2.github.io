Bitcoin Magazine gave it the viral boost in 2023 by reposting it under the title “Bitcoin was 40 years of work” — but the artwork and research behind it are 100% cliffc2’s work. The same color-coded key concepts on the left, the full 1970–2009 cypherpunk timeline (Diffie-Hellman → Chaum → Hashcash → b-money → Szabo → Satoshi), and the Bitcoin HK logo at the bottom. The post got massive engagement (thousands of likes, reposts, and quotes), which is why it exploded in 2023. After that, the community ran with the exact same template: People created “Ethereum and the rise of…” versions, Solana, Cardano, and other chain remixes.
